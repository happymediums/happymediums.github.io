About Guitaricons

Guitaricons is an interactive project by Jim Morrison for BDes Interaction Design & Creative Entreprenuership.

The project involves designing a family set of icons. The idea behind 'A Jimi Hendrix Experience' is to design icons of an icon.

Hope you enjoy.


Feel free to download the illustrated icons or outline versions. And if your feeling extra creative, you can download the 'Jimi Hendrix Experience' colouring book.
